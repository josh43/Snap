The Picture controller is responsible for all things related to taking pictures/videos and sending them to friends.

MiddleViewController
                     Basically a scroll view that allows the user to scroll up to the settings or back down to
                               a take a picture.
AVCAM:PictureViewController
                     I edited one of apple's demos so that it would work nicely with my project to take pictures
                               and videos.
ContentFinishedViewController
                     Control's the content after the user has taken a picture or video so that the person may send/post
                                to wherever they would like
PictureEditorView
                     Allow's you to draw and put text labels on images after being captured
SettingsViewController
                     Control's the settings, which does not do much at this time other than allow you to logout
                                and see your username
AccountSettingsTVC
                     It's an account settings table view controller ....
